O R I G I N S

As part of the quintessential college experience, we are surrounded by people who come from all different parts of the world and walks of life. The idea for our website came from fusing various parts of our interests together to ultimately have a platform that can allow travelers to become more concious of their footprints. We aim to accomplish this by providing suggestions for literature about countries that tourists can delve into, providing documentaries that depict the complex culture of countries as well, and finally including ways that would make tourism more sustainable.

 

F U T U R E

This project right now is contained within 20 countries simply due to the sheer amount of information that is available. In the future, we would like to expand to beyond 20 countries and promote sustainable tourism across the world. Furthermore, we are currently limiting movie genres to documentaries about the chosen country, but we hope to expand to all genres in the future.
